01 · Daily Rhythm

Fixed times, fixed content types — no decision fatigue on what to do when.

7:00 – 8:30am

Morning Session

Bible study first, then the day's topic — deep work while you're freshest. Take notes like you'll teach it tomorrow.

Noon · 45 min

Midday Block

Book + article. Read your daily book chunk (see the 📖 link on each day) and one article — no video here.

7:00pm

Evening Block

Video + article. Watch the day's 🎥 video and skim one more article. No book reading at night.

Bible reading happens every single day in the Morning Session — non-negotiable, separate from the curriculum below.
02 · Daily Scorecard

Score what you learn. Impact plus simplicity, out of ten.

Impact (1–5) + Simplicity (1–5) = Total /10
Impact: how much this moved your brand, business, or skills forward. Simplicity: how easily you actually grasped and applied it. Three low scores in a row means swap the resource — don't push through blindly.
Day 14 — No-code automation w/ Zapier — Impact 4, Simplicity 3, Total 7/10 — Shipped: automated my content calendar

05 · The Instagram Community

Learning in public, daily — the thing that carries people along with you.

Daily rhythm

One short post or reel a day from your Micro Block notes — "Today I learned X" in under 60 seconds.

Weekly ritual

A Sunday recap carousel — this week's lessons, what you built, your scorecard average.

Content pillars

Rotate: AI/tools · business lesson · behind-the-build · faith/mindset · Q&A with followers.

Engagement habit

Reply to every comment in the first 30 minutes after posting — that's what actually builds community.
